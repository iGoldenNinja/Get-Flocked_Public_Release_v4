SD CARD CLEAN SLATE - v4.30

Copy the contents of this folder to the root of a FAT32 microSD card.

- /sound_tones.csv controls startup, ready, alert, and geo tone sequences.
- /geo_index.csv and /geo_groups/ include the ready-to-use East Coast OSM-derived grouped geofence dataset.
- GPS/geofencing requires a compatible UART GPS module installed in the M5Stack FIRE module slot or connected to the configured GPS port. RF detection still works without GPS.
- No logs, saved configs, private/personal coordinates, or local exact-MAC watch lists are included.
- The device may create CSV logs here when Logging is enabled.
